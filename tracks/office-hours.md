# Office Hours

Self-paced doesn't have to mean alone. Office Hours are a recurring live call where you bring a
stuck lab, a half-working detection, or a "is this the right approach?" question — and work through
it with a maintainer, a mentor, and other learners on the same track.

They run in the **🎙 Office Hours** voice channel on the
[Plaintext Discord](https://discord.gg/sZjQYqVvG). Everything happens there: screen-share for the
hard parts, the **`#labs-and-docker`** and **`*-help`** forums for the threads that outlive the call.

## How it works

- **Open format.** No fixed lecture. We triage what people bring — env that won't `make up`, a
  Vulhub container exiting, a detection that won't fire, a capstone you want a second opinion on.
- **Bring a specific question.** "It doesn't work" gets "what doesn't?" Have the module/lab, the
  exact command and full error, and your OS/Docker version ready — same ask as the help forums.
- **Screen-share encouraged.** Most lab problems are five minutes once we can see the terminal.
- **It stays searchable.** Anything broadly useful gets written up in the matching `*-help` forum
  afterward so the next person finds it without needing the call.

## Between sessions

Office Hours are the live layer on top of the always-on community — they don't replace it:

- **Async help:** the per-track **`*-help`** forums and **`#labs-and-docker`** (the busiest channel).
  One question = one post, mark it ✅ Solved when sorted.
- **Study Rooms & Co-working:** the **📚 Study Room** and **🧑‍💻 Co-working** voice channels are open
  any time — grab a peer and pair on a lab without waiting for a scheduled call.
- **Show your work:** post finished capstones and phase projects in **`#wins`**, and they may land on
  the [Showcase](showcase.md).
